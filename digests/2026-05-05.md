## K-12 Education & Schools

Recent reports highlight ongoing efforts in Chicago K-12 education focused on teacher professional development and middle school improvement initiatives. Educators had opportunities to participate in sessions for Jewish American Heritage Month, designed to help teachers integrate diverse cultural histories into their curriculum and deepen students' understanding of identity. These "Learn and Earn" sessions aimed to provide insights for teachers across various subjects, including history and ethnic studies.

Additionally, the To&Through Middle Grades Network held its annual Celebration of Learning. This event brought together partner schools to reflect on processes for testing new ideas and the impact of co-creating these initiatives with their Middle Grades Network Student Fellowships. The focus was on collaborative innovation and incorporating student perspectives to enhance middle school educational experiences.
